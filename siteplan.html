Floripa Beach Guide

This name was chosen for being direct and informative, combining the popular term "Floripa" with "Guide" to clearly indicate the site's purpose as a beach tourist guide. Suggested domain: floripabeachguide.com

Site Purpose

The Floripa Beach Guide aims to be the definitive guide to Florianópolis beaches, offering visitors and residents:

  • Detailed information about each beach
  • Sectorized interactive maps
  • Tips on available activities and services
  • Real-time beach conditions
  • Environmental preservation guide

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"I'm a tourist planning my vacation. Which is the best beach for surfing in Florianópolis?"

Scenario 2:

"I'm a local resident looking for a quiet beach to take my family on weekends. Which beaches are most suitable for children?"

Scenario 3:

"I want to know which restaurants and kiosks are near the beach I plan to visit."
